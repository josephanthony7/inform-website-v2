# Inform Growth Website Design Plan (Infrastructure-Focused)

**Pages:** Home, Services, Case Studies, Contact
**Primary Goal:** Book discovery calls
**Tone:** Direct and urgent
**Visual Style:** 1930s Rubber Hose Animation + Die-cut vinyl sticker aesthetic (Infrastructure-focused, no mascot)

**Visual Approach:** Abstract plumbing infrastructure and modular icon system replace mascot-driven design. All visuals use isometric pipe systems, technical diagrams, and mechanical elements in rubber hose animation style.

## **Typography & Layout Specifications**

### **Font Usage**
- **H1 (Page Heroes):** Fraunces Bold, 48-64px, Carbon Gray or White (depending on background)
- **H2 (Section Headlines):** Fraunces Bold, 36-42px
- **H3 (Subsections):** Fraunces Bold, 24-28px
- **Body Copy:** Space Mono Regular, 16-18px, Vintage Cream on Carbon Gray backgrounds
- **CTA Buttons:** Space Mono Bold, 18px, Carbon Gray text on ROI Green background
- **Accent/Stats:** Space Mono Bold, 20-24px, ROI Green

### **Color Application**
- **Primary Background:** Carbon Gray (#2B2B2B)
- **Section Alternating:** Some sections can use slightly lighter gray (#3A3A3A) for subtle separation
- **CTAs:** ROI Green (#39FF14) with Carbon Gray text
- **Problem indicators:** Rust Orange (#D35400)
- **Success indicators:** ROI Green (#39FF14)
- **Body text:** Vintage Cream (#F5F5DC)
- **Borders/Accents:** White (#FFFFFF)

### **Layout Principles**
- **Mobile-first:** 72% of engagement is mobile
- **High contrast:** Ensures readability on small screens
- **Vertical scroll preferred:** Avoid horizontal layouts
- **White space:** Let sections breathe, don't overcrowd
- **Die-cut sticker aesthetic:** All infrastructure elements have bold white borders, appear to "pop" off background

## **Design Philosophy**

### **Why Infrastructure Focus Works**

**More Technical Authority:**
Abstract infrastructure illustrations position you as serious engineers, not consultants with cute mascots.

**Scalable & Flexible:**
Modular icon system is easier to expand than maintaining character consistency across hundreds of assets.

**Better Buyer Alignment:**
Technical buyers (CTOs, RevOps Directors, VPs of Sales) respond better to engineering diagrams than cartoon characters.

**Unique Differentiation:**
Most B2B sites use either stock photos or flat corporate illustrations. Technical infrastructure graphics in rubber hose style creates unique visual identity.

### **The Rubber Hose Style Advantage**

Even without a mascot character, the 1930s rubber hose animation aesthetic provides:
- **Instant Recognition:** Unique style stands out in crowded B2B market
- **Approachability:** Vintage cartoon style softens technical complexity
- **Brand Consistency:** Die-cut sticker aesthetic works across all asset types
- **Mobile Optimization:** Bold lines and high contrast ensure readability on small screens
