# Quick Background Removal Guide

If your infrastructure illustrations or icons include unwanted backgrounds, use the steps below to make them transparent.

---

## 🏆 **Best Option: remove.bg** (Recommended)

**Why:** Fastest, most accurate, completely automatic

**How to use:**
1. Go to **[remove.bg](https://remove.bg)**
2. Drag and drop your image (or click Upload)
3. Wait 5 seconds - AI automatically removes background
4. Click "Download" (free for web-resolution)
5. Done! You now have a transparent PNG

**Cost:** FREE for standard resolution (perfect for web)
**Time:** 5 seconds per image
**Quality:** Excellent - uses AI, works great on illustrations

---

## 🎨 **Option 2: Canva** (If you have a Canva account)

**Why:** You might already have Canva, background remover is built-in

**How to use:**
1. Go to **[canva.com](https://canva.com)**
2. Click "Edit Photo"
3. Upload your image
4. Click "Edit Photo" → "Background Remover"
5. Download as PNG

**Cost:** FREE with Canva Pro (or free trial)
**Time:** 10 seconds per image
**Quality:** Very good

---

## 🖌️ **Option 3: Photopea** (Free Photoshop alternative)

**Why:** Free, browser-based, works exactly like Photoshop

**How to use:**
1. Go to **[photopea.com](https://photopea.com)**
2. Click "Open from computer" → Upload your image
3. In the right panel, click the layer → Delete Background layer (if there is one)
4. OR: Select Magic Wand tool → Click gray background → Press Delete
5. File → Export As → PNG → Check "Transparency" → Save

**Cost:** FREE (has ads)
**Time:** 30 seconds per image
**Quality:** Perfect control, manual selection

---

## ⚡ **Option 4: Adobe Express** (Free Adobe tool)

**Why:** Made by Adobe, very reliable

**How to use:**
1. Go to **[adobe.com/express/feature/image/remove-background](https://www.adobe.com/express/feature/image/remove-background)**
2. Upload your image
3. AI removes background automatically
4. Download PNG

**Cost:** FREE (limited downloads per month)
**Time:** 10 seconds per image
**Quality:** Excellent

---

## 📋 **Batch Processing (If you have many images)**

**Option: PhotoScissors**
- Website: **[photoscissors.com](https://photoscissors.com)**
- Upload multiple images at once
- AI removes all backgrounds
- Download all as transparent PNGs
- **Cost:** Small fee for batch processing, but very fast

## 🎯 **My Recommendation:**

### For a batch of illustrations + icons:

**Use remove.bg:**
1. Go to remove.bg
2. Upload each image one by one (takes ~5 seconds each)
3. Download all 11 transparent PNGs
4. Replace your current images in `/public/infrastructure/` and `/public/icons/`
5. Total time: **~2-5 minutes for a full batch**

---

## ✅ **How to verify transparency:**

After downloading, open the PNG file:
- **Mac:** Open in Preview, should see checkered pattern behind character
- **Windows:** Open in Photos app, should see checkered pattern
- **Any OS:** Open in browser, drag to new tab - background should be white/transparent, not gray

---

## 🚫 **What NOT to do:**

- ❌ Don't try to code around this - CSS can't properly remove backgrounds
- ❌ Don't manually erase in Paint - too slow and messy
- ❌ Don't use low-quality background removers - use the tools above
